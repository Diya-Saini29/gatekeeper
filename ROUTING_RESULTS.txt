### 💡 KEY INSIGHTS

**What Works Well:**
- ✅ Simple, predictable queries → Cache (instant, free)
- ✅ Direct fact lookups → Cache
- ✅ Greetings and small talk → Cache
- ✅ Complex analysis → Expensive model (only when needed)

**What Could Improve:**
- Small model only used 10% of time - could tune thresholds
- Some borderline queries could be routed to small model
- More intent examples would improve accuracy further
